Who Are They?

Emotional Park Vandals are a three-piece noise-pop band from somewhere between your gut and your ribcage. They write songs about crying in public, yelling into vending machines, and the particular loneliness of a Tuesday afternoon.

They've been tearing up stages and feelings since their first show, which ended with a feedback loop and two broken strings. They consider that a success.

  • Chris — Bass, keyboards, vocals
  • Richard — Vocals, guitar
  • Thomas — Guitar, vocals, noises
